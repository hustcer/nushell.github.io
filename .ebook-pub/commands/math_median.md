# math median

**version**: 0.80.0

## **usage**:

Computes the median of a list of numbers.

## Signature

`> math median `

## Examples

Compute the median of a list of numbers

```bash
> [3 8 9 12 12 15] | math median
```

Compute the medians of the columns of a table

```bash
> [{a: 1 b: 3} {a: 2 b: -1} {a: -3 b: 5}] | math median
```
