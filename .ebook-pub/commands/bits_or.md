# bits or

**version**: 0.80.0

## **usage**:

Performs bitwise or for integers.

## Signature

`> bits or (target)`

## Parameters

- `target`: target integer to perform bit or

## Examples

Apply bits or to two numbers

```bash
> 2 | bits or 6
```

Apply logical or to a list of numbers

```bash
> [8 3 2] | bits or 2
```
