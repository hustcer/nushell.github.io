# math min

**version**: 0.80.0

## **usage**:

Finds the minimum within a list of numbers or tables.

## Signature

`> math min `

## Examples

Compute the minimum of a list of numbers

```bash
> [-50 100 25] | math min
```

Compute the minima of the columns of a table

```bash
> [{a: 1 b: 3} {a: 2 b: -1}] | math min
```
